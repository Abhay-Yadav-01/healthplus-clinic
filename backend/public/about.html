About Us

Your Family's Health Partner Since 2018

Our Story

HealthPlus Clinic was established in 2018 with a vision to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to the people of Gorakhpur and surrounding regions. What started as a small clinic with just three dedicated doctors has now grown into a multi-specialty healthcare facility serving over 50,000 patients annually.

Our commitment to patient care, combined with state-of-the-art medical facilities and a team of highly experienced doctors, has made us one of the most trusted healthcare providers in Eastern Uttar Pradesh. We believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone, and we strive to deliver compassionate care that treats not just the symptoms, but the whole person.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to every individual, ensuring that no one is denied proper medical care due to socio-economic barriers.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted healthcare provider in Eastern Uttar Pradesh, known for clinical excellence, patient-centric care, and continuous innovation in medical services.
